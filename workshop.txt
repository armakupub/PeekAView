title=Peek a View
description=[h1]Expand your vision[/h1]
description=
description=Project Zomboid hides a lot of what's actually visible to your character behind walls, trees, and floors above. PeekAView extends three rendering passes so your screen catches up with your character's eyes. Three independent features:
description=
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Wall cutaway[/b]: walls are cut away at greater range, so corners and doorways reveal what's behind them earlier as you approach.
description=[*][b]Tree fade[/b]: trees in your view become translucent. Spot zombies behind tree lines where line of sight reaches; safer driving with earlier hazard visibility.
description=[*][b]Stair view[/b]: the upper floor renders while you're climbing, so you see what's upstairs before you reach it.
description=[/list]
description=
description=Same rules as vanilla. No X-ray, no see-through walls, no enemy outlines. Your character's line of sight still decides what's actually visible.
description=
description=[h1]Settings[/h1]
description=Each feature is independent. Run all three together, just the one you need, or somewhere in between. Every feature has its own Enable toggle, and the wall and tree fade ranges are tunable in the Options menu.
description=
description=Options → Mods → Peek a View, four sections: Global, Wall cutaway, Tree fade, Stair view. [b]F8[/b] toggles the master Enable in-game (green/red halo confirms; rebindable in Options → Keybind under [noparse][PeekAView][/noparse]).
description=

description=[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
description=[list]
description=[*]Safe to add or remove mid-save: no save data touched. Visual effects clear on the next save reload after removing the mod from the mod list.
description=[*]Client-side only: each player who wants the effect needs the mod (and ZombieBuddy) installed.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h1]FAQ[/h1]
description=
description=[b]Does it conflict with the Staircast Workshop mod?[/b]
description=No. If both are subscribed, PeekAView detects Staircast at runtime and yields its own stair view so the two can run together.
description=
description=[b]Does it work in multiplayer?[/b]
description=Yes, client-side only. Every client also needs ZombieBuddy installed.
description=
description=[b]I disabled PeekAView in the mod list mid-session but the effects are still showing. What now?[/b]
description=Project Zomboid keeps mod code in memory across save reloads, so disabling PeekAView in the mod list mid-session doesn't shut it down on its own. Three ways to clear it: (1) toggle it off via the mod's own in-game settings (instant), (2) reload your save (PeekAView checks the active mod list on each save load and self-deactivates if it's no longer there), or (3) restart PZ. This is an already-reported framework-level limitation.
description=
description=[b]Does it affect performance?[/b]
description=Several caches keep the runtime cost close to vanilla. Standing still and walking on foot are the cheapest paths. Lower the range sliders if your hardware struggles.
description=
