title=Peek a View
description=[h1]See what your character sees[/h1]
description=
description=In vanilla PZ, walls and roofs fade out only within roughly 5 tiles of your character, and trees only within a small circle around the character. So the [i]player[/i] behind the screen consistently sees less than the [i]character[/i] has line of sight to: a zombie leaning on the back of a house, a fence corner you can't peek around, a tree directly in front of you on the road. [b]Peek a View[/b] extends those same vanilla fade mechanics further out, so what reaches your screen matches roughly what your character would already perceive.
description=
description=Same rules as vanilla, just triggered from further out. No X-ray, no see-through walls, no enemy outlines. A faded tree doesn't grant line of sight. Anything behind it still needs to be in your character's line of sight to appear, and a zombie standing partially on a bush or tree tile can still stay hidden. Vanilla's visibility rules still apply: closed doors, curtains, fog of war still hide what they always did.
description=
description=[h1]What this mod does[/h1]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Wall cutaway[/b]: extends vanilla's ~5-tile cutaway range up to 20 tiles (default 10).
description=[*][b]Tree fade[/b]: trees fade in the direction you're actively looking, up to 25 tiles. Outdoors only; indoors falls back to vanilla.
description=[*]Also fixes a B42 render bug where player-built structures cause nearby vanilla walls to disappear.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h1]Settings[/h1]
description=Options → Mods → Peek a View, three sections: Global, Wall cutaway, Tree fade. [b]F8[/b] toggles the mod on/off (green/red halo confirms; rebindable in Options → Keybind under [noparse][PeekAView][/noparse]).
description=
description=The experience can be tuned in-game — adjust ranges, restrict cutaway to aim-state only, or disable cutaway in vehicles.
description=

description=[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
description=[list]
description=[*]No known incompatibilities with other Project Zomboid mods at this time.
description=[*]Singleplayer + multiplayer (client-side rendering only, no server changes).
description=[*]Localized in EN, DE, FR, ES, RU, PL, PT-BR, IT, TR, CN, KO, JP.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h1]Trade-offs[/h1]
description=[list]
description=[*]Visual departure from TIS's intended look. If you prefer the vanilla style, this mod isn't for you.
description=[*]In dense city blocks, buildings can fade in and out more quickly as you move, which can be distracting at first.
description=[*]Per-frame cost is small but not zero. On lower-end hardware, dropping the ranges a few tiles helps.
description=[/list]
